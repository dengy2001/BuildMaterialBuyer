ATAPI-44 connector (used on 2.5" devices) has 4 additional pins used for power. Pins 1-40 are the same as the 40 pin connector, but the spacing between pins is smaller and they are not physically compatible.

80-wire cables has the same pinout as 40-conductor cables. Internally the connectors are different: the 80-wire cable provides one ground wire to each signal wire.

Cables usually come with three differently colored connectors (blue=controller, black=master drive, gray=slave drive)
